EAT LIKE YOU WOULD IN YOUR FAVOURITE RESTAURANT

We bring your favourite restaurant to the workplace. With a weekly menu rotation, you can enjoy a varied and completely new selection of lunch, breakfast, and snacks, all freshly prepared, every week.

Sample menu

NEW WEEK, NEW MENU

Variety is the spice of life, so whether looking for something hearty, Asian, traditional, vegan seasonal, or low carb, there is something for everyone at FELFEL.

Curated by our team of Food Scouts

From secret regional ingredients to the latest food trends, our Food Scouts make sure that only the best ingredients end up on your plate.

PREPARED BY ONLY THE BEST LOCAL CHEFS

We work primarily with Swiss family-run and independent small businesses, and our Food Scouts find the best local chefs from all across Switzerland.

A SMART FRIDGE

The selection in each fridge is data-driven to reflect employee preferences and restocked accordingly. This way we maximise variety and choice while minimising food waste.

MINIMISED FOOD WASTE

Staying true to our sustainability mission, our selection ensures that we provide based on what your team wants and minimises food waste.

Affordable

FELFEL is the affordable alternative to a canteen. It only needs 1m² of space and from day 1 all employees have access.

User-friendly

All employees have access from day one and payment for the meals is easily managed through our FELFEL app.

All-around service

Our all-around service for your company and employees includes daily deliveries, recycling, and support – all provided with passion.

  • Daily deliveries

    We fill the fridge with freshly made meals, snacks, and drinks daily to keep you and your team going - all without the hassle of ordering and organising delivery.

  • Recycling
  • Customer support
  • Events & Catering

PREPARED BY THE BEST LOCAL CHEFS

Our Food Scouts collaborate on menus with the best local chefs from all across Switzerland, meaning you get the best, most-innovative meals from culinary experts.

GIOVANNI

Brining authentic Italian dishes to the table, Sicilian chef Giovanni has been part of FELFEL since the beginning.

SAALÄND

Banding together with other small dairy farmers from Zurich Oberland, Albert produces the best mueslis and yoghurts.

SUPER NATURAL CLUB
Founded by Romain and Arnaud, these drinks use 100% natural and organic ingredients and are all produced Lausanne.

PAUL & LULU
Partner chefs from the beginning, Franz and Oli have been providing innovative, chef-driven creations that inspire.

LAITERIE DE GRANDVILLARD
Working with local dairy farmers, Laurent transforms milk from the Grandvillard region into creamy yoghurts.
