THE FOUNDING FAMILY

We are the Steiner family, Daniela and Emanuel. As a small, family-run company, FELFEL is close to our heart – our business baby, so to speak.

How did it all begin? With frustration! Emanuel was working in an office in the middle of Zurich and was unhappy: “There’s simply nothing good to eat! Take-away? Not tasty! Supermarket salad? Too expensive! Restaurant? Takes two hours! And worst of all: everyone splits up over lunch because they want to eat different things. This can’t be it – FELFEL needs to be created!

Together with over 100 FELFELians at our two locations in Lausanne and Zurich, as well as our partners throughout Switzerland, we are the FELFEL family – a sustainably managed company characterised by trust, commitment and a great deal of passion from each individual. Every day we are personally and actively committed to the satisfaction and well-being of our customers.

We look forward to spoiling you with culinary delights.

Yours,
It is still rumoured today that Emanuel’s fear of “eating alone” was the main reason behind founding FELFEL. Thanks to FELFEL employees in companies all over Switzerland can now enjoy lunch together.

THE FELFEL STORY

APRIL 2023

FELFEL commits to NET-ZERO by 2026

FELFEL sets an example for sustainable food production and announces its intention to be Net Zero by 2026.

DECEMBER 2022

FELFEL ACQUIRES SNX

FELFEL acquires its direct competitor Snx. With the acquisition, FELFEL strengthens its position as market leader in employee catering and pushes forward with its rapid growth.

OCTOBER 2021

FELFEL EXPANDS INTO OFFICE COFFEE

FELFEL expands and enters the office coffee business with the newly created concept “Gavetti”.

JUNE 2021

FELFEL TRIPLES ITS SECOND LOCATION SIZE IN LAUSANNE

Demand for FELFEL is also growing strongly in French-speaking Switzerland, therefore the team and office are doubled in size.

MARCH 2020

FELFEL DEVELOPS A HOME DELIVERY SERVICE

FELFEL uses the Corona pandemic as an opportunity to also offer a delivery service for FELFEL customers who are working from home.

APRIL 2019

FELFEL TOP-EMPLOYER IN ROMANDIE

FELFEL is amongst the best employers in Suisse Romande in the Small and Medium Companies category, according to the Magazine Bilan.

SEPTEMBER 2018

FELFEL WINS : E&Y YOUNG ENTREPRENEUR PRICE

On the evening of 28 October, over 100 companies awarded FELFEL with the prize in the category Emerging Entrepreneur.

MARCH 2018

FELFEL IS IN ROMANDIE!

FELFEL opens its tasting room in Lausanne. More and more companies offer good food at work in Romandie.

FEBRUARY 2018

AWARD: SEF HIGH POTENTIAL

The SEFs jury awards and grants FELFEL with the label SEF High Potential.

JUNE 2017

FELFEL WINS SWISS ECONOMIC FORUM AWARD

In Interlaken, FELFEL wins the “Best Startup Award”.

FEBRUARY 2014

FIRST CUSTOMER

FELFEL is created. The team installs its first intelligent fridge at a customer location in Basel.

FELFEL is the most popular office food and coffee solution in Switzerland. Since 2014, we have helped more than 700 companies of all sizes increase employee satisfaction and company culture.
